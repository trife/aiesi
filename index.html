AI and Image Data for Plant Sciences

An afternoon workshop on two ways to make a computer "see" a plant.

Hub Materials Demos Teachable Machine Learned vs. Rules

Agenda

Part 1 - Do the AI science ~75 min
  • ~35 min

    Browser demos

    Start with the "rules you write" side. Work through all four demos: adjust parameters to change results and compare to ground truth.

  • ~25 min

    Teachable Machine

    Now flip to "learned from examples." Train a classifier in the browser, then break it with edge cases.

  • ~15 min

    Name the contrast

    Learned vs. rules, where each broke, and why validation is unavoidable.

Part 2 - Discuss the science ~75 min
  • ~30 min

    Adapt the trust check

    Rewrite the six-question trust check for your own subject.

  • ~30 min

    Design one activity

    In small groups, build or retrofit one classroom exercise where AI does the grunt work but the graded skill is judgment.

  • ~15 min

    Share-out

    Groups present their activity design.
